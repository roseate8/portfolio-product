# Content Strategy: Portfolio Image Assets

## 1. The Root & Meta-Layer
*The entry point. Sets the tone of "Builder + Strategist."*

### **Node: Rudram Piplad (Root)**
*   **Image 1: The Persona (Candid)**
    *   **What:** A high-quality candid shot. You at a whiteboard, holding a VR headset, or working at a laptop. Monochrome or high contrast works well.
    *   **Why:** Signals approachability and "action," not just a corporate suit.
*   **Image 2: The Mental Model (Graphic)**
    *   **What:** A sleek, stylized SVG or PNG abstracting your portfolio graph structure.
    *   **Why:** Reinforces that the *structure* of this website is a deliberate product choice.

### **Node: Information**
*   **Image 1: The Stack (Visual List)**
    *   **What:** A clean grid of logos for your core tools (Python, Unity, Figma, LangChain, TypeScript).
    *   **Why:** Allows technical recruiters to scan your stack in 2 seconds without reading.
*   **Image 2: Contact Card**
    *   **What:** A QR code or a clean vCard graphic.
    *   **Why:** Utility.

### **Node: Resume**
*   **Image 1: The Artifact**
    *   **What:** A crisp thumbnail of the actual PDF document.
    *   **Why:** Acts as a tangible anchor for the download link.
